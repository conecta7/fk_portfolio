project goal

The goal was to create a seamless social media experience specifically curated for dog owners. This project was started from scratch, from research and initial sketches up to prototyping, branding and final design.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer

User Research, Visual Design, Prototyping, Brand Design and Copywriting.
