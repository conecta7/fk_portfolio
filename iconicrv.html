IconicRV

Solar Builder Web-App

project goal

IconicRV is a solar implementation company that helps RV owners integrate a solar system in their RVs. What we needed to create was a virtual calculator that enables users to generate their own solar system based on their current RV setup and power consumption. I also designed their landing page.

team members

Project Manager, Front End Developer, Back End Developer, UX/UI Designer, Graphic Designer

ROLE

UX/UI Designer

User Research, Visual Design, Prototyping and Copywriting.

View Live Project

We kicked this project off by cleaning up the pre-existing wireframes to be able to shape a proper model of how the app should look like and the base functionalities. Our goal was to create a model of this web-app that incorporates all of the needed data and have a seamless design and be simplistic for easy comprehension so the calculator could provide accurate results.

wireframes

The design I tried to keep as ‘lightweight’ as possible due to the main focus was on the web-app itself, so, multiple CTAs, clear guidelines, short product descriptions were crucial to the marketing side of the brand itself. The subtle graphics and illustrations were just a cherry on the top.

design

This project took a very coordinated approach to be accomplished. It boasted positive results, gaining a large increase in sales and site activity. Competitors have yet to catch up with our web-app due to its efficiency and reliability. If I were to do this project again I would focus a bit more on the UI and the illustration side of things but overall I’m happy with how it turned out.
